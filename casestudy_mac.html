MAC

Brief

MAC Cosmetics is a well known and distributed brand within the cosmetic industry. They provide products to everyone from novices to professional makeup artists. The goal of this project was to redesign the look and feel of their website during Fall 2016's WNM 606: Principles in UX class under Instructor Justine Luis. By making it more appealing and user-friendly. This meant removing the space-age typeface, uncluttering the homepage, toning down the dark background as well as making the products standout on there own.

Typeface

Aa

Paul D. Hunt developed a unique typeface for Adobe Systems first open-source typeface called Source Sans Pro. It’s a family consisting of 6 weights - extra-light, light, regular, semibold, bold, and black - with a wide support system across the globe.

The typeface is well matched to the MAC brand with it’s large x-heights and character widths, making it ideally optimized for user interfaces and legibility.

Technology

Wireframes were created using pen and paper before being uploaded in Adobe Illustrator. iPhone 6/7 were created in Adobe XD to be viewed on a mobile device. Then mockups were created in Adobe Illustrator using Adobe’s desktop templates.
