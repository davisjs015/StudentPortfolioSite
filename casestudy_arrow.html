Arrow

Brief

The Arrow project was designed as a television bumper animation for Spring 2017's WNM 613: Topics in Motion Graphics under Instructor Michael Ritchie. The goal of the project was to create a television bumper animation using any television show, series or even movie. The project had to be animated for roughly 30 seconds and fit within the show and/or movie.

Typeface

The typeface chosen for the Arrow Project was Cochin. The type was designed by Georges Peignot. Cochin is a serif typeface with small x-heights and long ascenders making the words more recognizable. The typeface fits with the overall feel of the show and mimics the black letter typeface you associate with medieval times and Robin Hood.

Technology
