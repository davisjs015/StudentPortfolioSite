The Market

Brief

The market has a vast inventory ranging from everyday food items to electronics to car parts. To keep the site easy to use the goal was to create a responsive website using a grid system to keep everything inline. The user has access to the companies contact information and FAQS about the company via their respective pages I the navigation bar located in the header. The goal of this project was to make a responsive inventory website during Fall 2016's semester for WNM 608: Mobile Web Technology 1 under Instructor Kevin Smit, using HTML5, CSS3, PHP, MySQL using products created or chosen by fellow classmates.

Typeface

Aa

For The Market two typefaces were Lato and Merriweather due to the contrast of the sans serif and serif type qualities. Lukasz Dziedzic designed the Lato type to be a classically proportioned yet elegant sans serif. Lato has a standard x-height that makes it easy to read and creates a harmonious feel when paired with the Merriweather type. Merriweather was designed by Sorkin Type. With large x-heights with robust serifs for easy reading across the screen.
