Resort and Spa

Brief

Resort and Spa is a UX/UI design created to show the responsiveness of their reservation page.

The goal and plan for this project was to create the reservation page of a resort in the mountains using unique imagery and designs. The design needed to be responsive and showcase the overall look and feel of the reservation page on a desktop and/or laptop, tablet and a mobile device. Images used were taken from my personal collection of Mt. Rainier from the Olympia, Washington side.

Typeface

Aa

Due to the nature of the product the goal was to keep the typeface elegant and classic but rugged. This is why Optima, a sans serif and Palatino, an old-style serif were used. Both typefaces were designed by Herman Zapf and share one major characteristic, the low x-height high allowed the design to maintain a cohesion from header to footer. The legibility they both provide makes the type easy to read on all screens sizes which was ideal for the responsiveness required.

Technology

The wireframes are pen and paper drawings used for the prototype creations. Responsive

To keep the integrity of the desktop designs for the Resort and Spa the plan was to mimic the header image on all screen sizes and make the menu responsive by adding a hamburger as the screen size reduced to a phone.
