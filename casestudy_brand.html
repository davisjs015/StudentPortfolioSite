Brand

Brief

The brand project was designed to create a unique and personal identity for yourself within your chosen career path. The goal of this project was to design a brand story from start to finish that encompassed the story of the designer. The brand had to have a color palette, motto, logo, and a portfolio website to showcase the designers work.

Typeface

The typeface chosen for the brand project was Lato. The type was designed by Lukasz Dziedzic. The name Lato comes from the word for summer in Polish as Dziedzic designed the typeface in the summer. The classic proportions, elegance and seriousness that Lato brings makes this the perfect typeface for the brand project. It speaks to the designers need for stability, seriousness, and elegance within their designs.

Technology

The project was designed using Adobe Illustrator for the brand base such as the moodboard, words, and logo designs. Low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes were created using Adobe Illustrator to keep them cohesive and easily managed.
