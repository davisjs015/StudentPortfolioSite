Starbucks

Brief

The Starbucks project was designed as animated logo design for branding. The goal was to animate a logo for a business by creating a short animation. The project had to be 15-30 seconds in length to showcase you're understanding of Adobe After Effects.

Typeface

There was no new typeface used as it was already on the vector logo provided for the projects use. The typeface Starbucks does use is Freight Sans Black designed by Joshua Darden.

Technology

The logo was a vector provided for the project. To animate the logo Adobe Illustrator was used to convert it to layers for easy importing into Adobe After Effects. To create the animation Adobe After Effects was used. The mockups were created in Adobe Illustrator using Adobeâ€™s desktop templates.
